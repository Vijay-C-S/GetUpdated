About GetUpdated

Our Story

GetUpdated was born out of a simple idea ? to create a single platform where people can find everything they need to stay informed and grow in the digital age. In a world overflowing with information, we recognized the need for a curated, trustworthy source that brings together tech news, career opportunities, income ideas, and money-saving deals.

Founded in 2025 and based in Bangalore, India, we started as a small project and have grown into a platform that helps thousands of users every day. Our team is passionate about technology, career growth, and helping people make smarter financial decisions.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals with timely, accurate, and actionable information that helps them advance in their careers, stay updated with technology, and make smarter financial decisions.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted platform for job seekers, tech enthusiasts, and anyone looking to grow personally and financially in the digital economy.
