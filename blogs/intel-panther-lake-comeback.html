Intel Panther Lake CPU Review: Call It a Comeback

January 27, 2026 by GetUpdated Team

Intel's Core Ultra X9 "Panther Lake" 388H laptop processor marks a significant turning point for the company. After years of losing ground to AMD and Apple Silicon, Intel is finally delivering a chip that can genuinely compete � and in some cases, lead.

A New Era for Intel Laptops

The Panther Lake architecture represents Intel's most ambitious laptop processor in years. Built on an improved manufacturing process, the new chip addresses the efficiency and performance concerns that plagued previous generations.

Performance Highlights

In our testing, the Core Ultra X9 388H delivered impressive results across both single-threaded and multi-threaded workloads:

  • CPU Performance: Up to 35% faster than Meteor Lake in multi-threaded tasks
  • GPU Performance: Integrated graphics rival entry-level discrete GPUs
  • AI Acceleration: Enhanced NPU delivers significant improvements in AI workloads
  • Power Efficiency: Better performance-per-watt than previous generations

Real-World Testing

We tested the Panther Lake chip in the new Asus Zenbook Duo (2026), which showcases the processor's capabilities perfectly:

Productivity

For everyday tasks like web browsing, document editing, and video conferencing, the chip is incredibly snappy. Applications launch quickly, and multitasking feels smooth even with dozens of tabs and apps open.

Creative Work

Video editing in Adobe Premiere Pro and photo editing in Lightroom showed marked improvements. Export times were reduced significantly, and real-time preview performance was noticeably better.

Gaming

While not a replacement for discrete graphics, the integrated GPU handles casual gaming and older titles surprisingly well. Esports titles can run at 1080p with reasonable settings.

The AI Factor

Intel has significantly upgraded the Neural Processing Unit (NPU) in Panther Lake. This dedicated AI accelerator handles local AI tasks efficiently, which is increasingly important as more software incorporates AI features.

"Panther Lake represents everything Intel has been working toward. It's efficient, powerful, and finally competitive with the best in the market." - Tech Analyst

Supply Concerns

Despite the strong launch, Intel is reportedly facing supply constraints. The company is allocating more of its production capacity to money-making server chips, which could limit availability of Panther Lake laptops in the near term.

The Verdict

Intel's Panther Lake is the comeback the company desperately needed. It's not perfect � battery life, while improved, still trails Apple Silicon � but it's genuinely competitive in a way Intel laptops haven't been in years. For Windows users, Panther Lake laptops represent the best Intel has offered in a long time.

Key Takeaways

  • Significant performance improvements across the board
  • Better power efficiency than previous Intel generations
  • Enhanced AI capabilities with improved NPU
  • Supply may be limited initially
  • Strong value proposition for Windows laptop buyers
