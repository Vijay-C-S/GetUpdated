?? How to Check Your VTU 5th Semester Result

Follow these simple steps to check your examination results:

  1. Click on the "Check Result" button above or visit results.vtu.ac.in
  2. Enter your University Seat Number (USN) in the given field
  3. Enter the CAPTCHA code shown on the screen
  4. Click on "Submit" button
  5. Your result will be displayed on the screen
  6. Download or print your result for future reference

??? About VTU

Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) is one of the largest technological universities in India. Established in 1998, it is named after the renowned Indian engineer Sir Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya. The university is headquartered in Belagavi (formerly Belgaum), Karnataka.
