Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I apply for multiple scholarships?

Yes! There's no restriction on applying for multiple scholarships. However, if you receive one government scholarship, you may not be eligible for another government scholarship simultaneously. Private scholarships usually don't have this restriction.

Q: Is there an age limit for scholarships?

Age limits vary by scholarship. Most UG scholarships require students to be under 25, while PG scholarships may allow up to 30-35 years. Always check specific eligibility criteria.

Q: Do I need to pay back scholarships?

No! Scholarships are grants, not loans. You don't need to repay them. However, some may have conditions like maintaining a minimum CGPA or completing your course.

Q: How can I increase my chances of getting a scholarship?

Focus on academics, participate in extracurriculars, write compelling SOPs, apply early, and apply to multiple scholarships across different categories.
