Camera System: ZEISS Excellence

The Nokia 12 Sirocco continues Nokia's legendary partnership with ZEISS optics. The quad-camera system includes:

  • 200MP Main Sensor: Captures incredibly detailed photos with excellent dynamic range
  • 50MP Ultra-wide: Perfect for landscapes and group shots
  • 12MP Telephoto: 3x optical zoom for portraits
  • 8MP Periscope: 10x optical zoom for long-range photography

Nokia's PureView technology returns with advanced computational photography, including AI-powered night mode, pro-level manual controls, and 8K video recording at 30fps.

One of the most exciting features of the Nokia 12 Sirocco is the included Nokia Stylus Pro.
